Kodeworks Gamejam Novemeber 2019

Kodeworks invites to Gamejam 15th to the 17th of November in Fjordgata 30, Trondheim!

We gather in our office to make games in a 48 hour time period based around a common theme. We brainstorm ideas together, present them to each other and gather around the projects we find most interesting. The most important thing is to have fun, collaborate and learn! Bring your own device and use whichever technology appeals to you.
